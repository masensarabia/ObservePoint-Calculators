Purpose:

This calculator is designed to help organizations evaluate the time and cost savings achieved by using ObservePoint's automated scanning solution compared to manual employee testing.

How It Works:

Users input the details related to their current manual testing process and ObservePoint's automated scanning process. The calculator then computes the total time and cost for both methods, highlighting the potential savings in time and cost when switching to automated scanning.

By using this calculator, organizations can make informed decisions about optimizing their testing processes, improving efficiency, and reducing costs.
